About the Project

The Point of Interest (POI) broker is developed and maintained at CITEVA (Centro de Investigación, Tecnología, Educación y Vinculación Astronómica), within the Universidad de Antofagasta, Chile.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between massive alert streams like ZTF and LSST and actionable astronomical research by providing efficient filtering and visualization tools.
